Explore the battlegrounds and master every corner

HAVEN

Location: Thimphu, Bhutan

Haven features three bomb sites instead of the standard two, creating unique strategic challenges. The map's monastery setting provides a mix of tight corridors and open courtyards.

Strategic Points:

  • Long C offers dangerous sightlines for snipers
  • The central B site is vulnerable to flanks from both sides
  • Garage serves as a critical connector between C and B
  • A site has multiple entry points requiring careful coverage

LOTUS

Location: Venice, Italy

Inferno is a tight urban landscape featuring narrow passageways, complex angles, and numerous hiding spots. The map's rustic Italian architecture provides a beautiful backdrop for intense firefights.

Strategic Points:

  • Mid control is crucial for team rotations
  • Banana corridor creates chokepoint dynamics
  • A site apartments offer vertical advantage
  • Ancient ruins provide unique cover and ambush spots

PEARL

Location: Tokyo, Japan

Pearl features a futuristic research facility with a unique day/night cycle mechanic. Every few rounds, lighting conditions change, affecting visibility and tactics across the map.

Strategic Points:

  • Central Observatory offers high-ground advantage
  • Underground tunnels provide safe rotation paths
  • Light-reactive doors only open during "day" cycles
  • Shadow zones offer stealth advantages during "night" cycles

FRACTURE

Location: Marrakech, Morocco

Mirage is set in an ancient desert city with dynamic sand elements. Parts of the map can transform as sand shifts, revealing new pathways or blocking existing ones throughout the match.

Strategic Points:

  • Palace offers protected sightlines to A site
  • Shifting dunes can provide unexpected cover
  • Underground cistern provides a hidden flank route
  • Bazaar area creates complex engagement patterns

FROST

Location: Anchorage, Alaska

Frost introduces environmental hazards with its arctic research station setting. Icy surfaces affect movement speed, and certain areas feature breakable ice that creates new tactical opportunities.

Strategic Points:

  • Central Greenhouse offers visibility but limited cover
  • Ice Lake provides risky but fast rotation options
  • Research Lab features multiple vertical levels
  • Thermal vents provide unique sound cues
